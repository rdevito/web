About Me

Hi there! My name is Roberta De Vito and I am a postdoctoral fellow at Princeton University in the department of Computer Science, advised by Barbara Engelhardt. Previously, I was a former visiting PhD Students at Dana-Farber Cancer Institute and Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health under the supervision of Giovanni Parmigiani and Lorenzo Trippa. I graduated at University of Padua in Statistical Science in 2016.

Resarch & Publications

The focus of my research is to develop statistical modeling for the analysis of big and high-throughput data, such as epidemiological and genomic data analysis. In this big picture, I use Bayesian approach and machine learning techniques to investigate biological issues, such as cancer and depression.
